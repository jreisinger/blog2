Find geolocation of an IP address

  • June 5, 2020
  • prog  
  • sec  
  • sysadmin  
  • go  
  • shell  

I wrote a little tool called checkip that finds out geographic location of an IP address. It's using the MaxMind's free GeoLite2 City database.

> checkip 1.1.1.1
city unknown, Australia, AU

> checkip $(dig +short reisinge.net)
Frankfurt am Main, Germany, DE

> checkip $(curl -s util.reisinge.net/addr)
Partizanska lupca, Slovakia, SK
